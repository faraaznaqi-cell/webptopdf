1. Introduction

Welcome to webptopdf.com ("Website", "Service", "we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our online image conversion tools.

By using our Website, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Key Privacy Feature: All image processing happens locally in your browser using Blob technology. No files are uploaded to our servers.

2. No Data Collection Policy

We want to be perfectly clear: We do not collect, store, or process any personal data, images, or files on our servers.

2.1 What We Don't Collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, location data
  • Uploaded Files: Images, PDFs, or any files you process through our tools
  • Browsing Data: IP addresses, browser history, cookies for tracking
  • Usage Data: How you use our tools, which features you access
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type

3. How Our Service Works

Our image conversion tools operate entirely within your web browser using client-side technologies:

  • All file processing happens locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or third-party servers
  • Files are processed in your browser's memory and deleted when you close the tab
  • We use only open-source client-side libraries (PDF-lib, FileSaver.js)
  • Batch processing for up to 10 files happens entirely in your browser

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that operate entirely client-side:

  • Google Fonts: For font display (no user data collection)
  • Font Awesome: For icons (no user data collection)
  • CDN Libraries: For JavaScript functionality (local processing only)

5. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies, web beacons, or any tracking technologies. Our Website does not:

  • Set any cookies on your device
  • Use local storage or session storage
  • Employ any analytics or tracking scripts
  • Display third-party advertisements

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we are compliant with children's privacy regulations including COPPA.

7. Data Security

Since no data leaves your device, your information remains secure. However, we recommend:

  • Using updated browsers with security features enabled
  • Avoiding processing sensitive or confidential documents on public computers
  • Keeping your antivirus software updated

8. Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, you don't need to exercise data rights such as:

  • Right to access (we have no data to access)
  • Right to deletion (we have no data to delete)
  • Right to rectification (we have no data to correct)
  • Right to data portability (we have no data to transfer)
