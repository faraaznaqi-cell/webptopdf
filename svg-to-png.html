Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How many SVG files can I convert at once?
You can convert up to 100 SVG files simultaneously, with a total size limit of 50MB. For larger batches, you can process them in multiple sessions.
What resolution should I choose for PNG output?
For most uses, "High Quality (2x)" is recommended. For printing or high-resolution displays, choose "Ultra HD (3x)" or "4K Quality (4x)". For original size, choose "Original Size (1x)".
Will converting to PNG affect image quality?
SVG is vector format (infinitely scalable), while PNG is raster format (fixed resolution). Our converter creates high-resolution PNGs that maintain excellent quality for most uses.
Can I convert complex SVG graphics with gradients and transparency?
Yes! Our converter handles complex SVG features including gradients, transparency, filters, and advanced vector elements, preserving them in the PNG output.
Are my files secure during conversion?
Absolutely! All processing happens locally in your web browser. Your files never leave your computer and are not stored on any server.
What if my SVG has embedded images or external resources?
Our converter processes self-contained SVG files best. SVGs with external dependencies may not render completely. For best results, use SVGs with all resources embedded.
