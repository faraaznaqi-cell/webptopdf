What Does It Mean to Change SVG to PNG?

When you change SVG to PNG, you're converting scalable vector graphics into a universal raster format. SVG files use mathematical equations to create images that can scale infinitely without quality loss, while PNG files use a grid of pixels to represent images with excellent quality and transparency support.

This conversion process transforms your vector-based artwork into a widely compatible image format that works across all devices, browsers, and applications.
